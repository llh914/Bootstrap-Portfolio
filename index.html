About Me

I'm a Chicago native with a Bachelor's in Biological Sciences from Northwestern University, currently working as a paralegal in an intellectual property law firm.


Over the last two years I have assisted many of our clients in securing patents for their inventions and designs, but now I am interested in transitioning to a career heavy in problem-solving and with a lot of room for creativity. Cue my interest in web development. I am currently enrolled in the Northwestern Coding Bootcamp program, and by the end of the program, I will be able to call myself a full-stack developer.


When I am not working or coding, you can find me kickboxing, trying out new restaurants, or traveling the world.
